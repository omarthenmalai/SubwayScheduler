MTA Subway

Facts about MTA Subway

The MTA Subway has Subway lines operating across New York - New Jersey including: Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn, Manhattan.

The longest line from the MTA Subway is: A. This Subway line starts from Far Rockaway - Mott Av [A] (Queens) and ends at Inwood - 207 St [A] (Manhattan). It covers over 51 km and has 59 stops.

The shortest line is: S. This Subway line begins from Times Sq - 42 St [N,Q,R,S,1,2,3,7,7x] (Manhattan) and finishes at Grand Central - 42 St [4,5,6x,6,7,7x,S] (Manhattan). It runs through 1 km with 2 stops.

According to Moovit Insights, the most popular lines for MTA Subway are J, B, L, 6, C.
