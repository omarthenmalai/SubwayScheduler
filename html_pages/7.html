Map terminology

\n\n \n \n
\n
\n
  • Island platform: A single platform, one or two, accessed by the same stairway or elevator in between two tracks providing service in either direction.
    • \n
  • Side platform: A platform positioned to the side of a pair of tracks. There many also be two separated platforms, access by separate sets of stairs or elevators, on either of the track.
    • \n
  • Terminal station: The first or last station on a line.
    • \n
  • Local service: A train which always operates and stops at every station on the line.
    • \n
  • Express service: A train that does not always operate or sometimes skips a station.
    • \n
  • ADA accessible stations: Stations that have features to accommodate customers with disabilities.
    • \n
